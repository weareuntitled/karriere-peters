Tier 2: Alias Tokens (Semantic Meaning)

Surface Colors

--surface-primary Semantic: Main surface
→ var(--color-slate-50)
--surface-container Semantic: Container
→ var(--color-white-alpha-75)
--glass-bg-medium Semantic: Glass material
→ var(--color-white-alpha-75)

Text Colors

--text-primary Semantic: Primary text
→ var(--color-slate-900)
--text-secondary Semantic: Secondary text
→ var(--color-slate-700)
--text-link Semantic: Links
→ var(--color-blue-500)

Brand Colors

--brand-primary Semantic: Brand color
→ var(--color-blue-500)
--brand-primary-dark Semantic: Dark brand
→ var(--color-blue-600)
--brand-primary-container Semantic: Container
→ var(--color-blue-100)
